Experiencing Heaven on Earth at Church

From the publisher

"Do I have to go to church?" Whether we've fielded this question from others or wondered it ourselves, pastor Adriel Sanchez invites us to consider afresh why Sunday corporate worship matters. Why We Worship reenchants our spiritual imagination, helping us rediscover the beauty of worship in Scripture and draw on the richness of Christian liturgy through the ages.

Sanchez delves into the biblical underpinnings and historical development of the core parts of a worship service — music, the sermon, Communion, baptism, confession, prayer, and the benediction. Church isn't meant to be a dull routine or an entertainment-driven spectacle, he argues; it's a weekly heavenly ascent that transforms us to live the Christian life here on earth.

— Nancy Guthrie, author and Bible teacher

About the author. Adriel Sanchez is senior minister at North Park Presbyterian Church in San Diego and the host of the Core Christianity podcast with Sola Media. He is the author of Praying with Jesus: Getting to the Heart of the Lord's Prayer and has written for Christianity Today, Modern Reformation, Credo Magazine, and Tabletalk. He lives with his wife and six children in Southern California.
