Knowing Christianity

The Foundations of the Christian Faith and How They Shape Daily Life

J. I. Packer · foreword by Gavin Ortlund

IVP · Theology / Christian Living · Paperback, 192 pages
Published July 21, 2026 · ISBN 9781514017180 · $26.99

Buy at Amazon Bookshop.org Christianbook

From the publisher

In a culture that grasps at the latest self-improvement plan or trendy spiritual experiences, J. I. Packer, author of Knowing God, turns our attention to realities far better than anything our self-absorbed society can offer. In Knowing Christianity, he lays out his grand panorama of the magnificent landscape we call the Christian faith, centering on the bedrock truths of Christianity and aiming to help ordinary believers deepen their devotion into discipleship.

The book covers key elements of spiritual life, Christian living, and our ultimate end — the doctrines of God, Christ, and the Holy Spirit; spiritual growth; prayer and worship; fellowship and renewal in the church; citizenship as a Christian; and the afterlife, God's judgment, and Christ's ascension. This new edition features a foreword by Gavin Ortlund, introducing Packer's enduring work to a new generation of readers.

About the author. J. I. Packer (1926–2020) is regarded as one of the most well-known theologians of our time. Once named to Time magazine's list of the 25 Most Influential Evangelicals in America, he served as Board of Governors' Professor of Theology at Regent College in Vancouver. His books include Knowing God, A Quest for Godliness, Evangelism and the Sovereignty of God, and Rediscovering Holiness.
