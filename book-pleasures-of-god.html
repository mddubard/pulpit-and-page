From the publisher

Christians long to know God — usually by studying his words and tracing his works. But there is another pathway into God's wonders: not the pathway of God's deeds but his delights. What a person enjoys most deeply shows the deep things of their character. So it is with God; what makes God glad reveals more of his glory than most of us ever dreamed.

In this beloved classic, pastor and bestselling author John Piper searches Scripture to uncover the pleasures that delight the heart of God — the glory of his Son, the beauty of creation, the richness of his grace, the prayers of his people, and more. As we behold God, we are transformed into his likeness, and our joy unites with his. Ideal for individual or group study.

Description adapted from the publisher, Crossway.
