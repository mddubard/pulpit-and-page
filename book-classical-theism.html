From the publisher

In Classical Theism: A Christian Introduction, Jordan L. Steffaniak retrieves the rich, historical doctrine of God that has guided the church throughout the ages. Classical theism fuels the life and worship of the church, but it is sometimes misunderstood as abstract and unwelcome, owing more to pagan philosophy than the words of the Bible. Steffaniak grounds classical theism in Scripture as guided by creeds and tradition, writing with a pastoral goal: understanding the God we worship.

Description adapted from the publisher, Lexham Press.

About the author. Jordan L. Steffaniak (PhD, University of Birmingham) is president of the London Lyceum and publisher for Hanover Press.
